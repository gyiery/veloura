Shipping Policy

We ensure a smooth and secure delivery experience right to your doorstep.

Domestic Shipping

Currently, Veloura delivers orders only within India. We partner with trusted courier services (BlueDart, Delhivery, XpressBees) to ensure your package reaches you safely and on time.

🚚 Free Standard Shipping on all prepaid orders above ₹999!

Shipping Charges & Timelines

Delivery times are calculated from the date of order dispatch, not the date the order is placed. Please note that during sale periods or festive seasons, deliveries may take an additional 1-2 days.

  • Standard Delivery (3-7 Business Days): ₹99 (Free above ₹999)
  • Express Delivery (2-3 Business Days): ₹199 (Available only in select metro cities)

Order Dispatch

We process and dispatch orders within 24-48 hours of placing the order (excluding Sundays and public holidays). For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, verification may take up to an additional 24 hours.

Order Tracking

As soon as your order is dispatched, you will receive an Email and SMS containing your tracking number and a direct link to track your package in real-time. You can also view the tracking status under "My Orders" in your Veloura account.

Address & Pincode Verification

Please ensure you provide a complete and accurate shipping address with the correct 6-digit PIN code. Incomplete or incorrect addresses may result in delayed or returned shipments. If a shipment is returned due to an incorrect address provided by the customer, a reshipping fee will be charged.

Undelivered Packages

Our courier partners will attempt delivery up to 3 times at your provided address. If the package is still undelivered after 3 attempts, it will be automatically returned to our warehouse. A refund will be processed (minus the original shipping costs) within 5-7 business days.

Force Majeure Events

Veloura is not liable for any delays caused by unforeseen circumstances beyond our control, including but not limited to: extreme weather conditions, natural disasters, political disruptions, pandemics, or courier strikes.
